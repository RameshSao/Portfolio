Cookies Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2025

1. Introduction

At UXDraw.com, we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, understand usage patterns, and personalize content. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and enhance your user experience.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand how visitors interact with the site.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services (e.g., analytics, embeds).

4. Who Collects Cookies?

  • Your Website (First-Party Cookies): No current use, may be added later for preferences.
  • Third-Party Services:
    • Font Awesome: Performance tracking - Font Awesome
    • Google Analytics: Analytics cookies like _ga, _gid - Google Analytics
    • EmailJS: Contact forms - EmailJS
    • UserWay: Accessibility preferences - UserWay
    • Social Links: LinkedIn, Behance, Dribbble
    • Vanilla Tilt.js / ScrollReveal / Particles.js: UI/UX enhancement tools

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies via your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect your experience. See guides for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
